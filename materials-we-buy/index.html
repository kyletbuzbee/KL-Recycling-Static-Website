What We Don't Buy / Prohibited Items

For safety and environmental reasons, we cannot accept the following materials:

Hazardous Materials

  • Radioactive materials
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Mercury-containing items (thermostats, switches)
  • Flammable or explosive materials
  • Pressurized tanks or containers (propane, oxygen)

Electronics & Batteries

  • Televisions and computer monitors (CRTs)
  • Household batteries (alkaline, NiCad)
  • Lithium-ion batteries
  • Items containing PCBs (transformers, capacitors)

Other Prohibited Items

  • Tires
  • Liquids of any kind
  • Garbage, wood, or excessive non-metallic materials
  • Stolen materials (we cooperate with law enforcement)

If you are unsure about an item, please contact us before bringing it to our facility.
