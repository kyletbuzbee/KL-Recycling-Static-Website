56 Years of Family Commitment

Three generations of Buzbees building relationships and recycling dreams

1956

Humble Beginnings

The Wells family opens its first scrap metal recycling yard in East Texas, driven by a commitment to community and fair business practices.

"We started with nothing but a strong work ethic and a promise to treat people right."

- The Buzbee Family

1970s

Family Legacy Continues

Under young leadership, K&L expands operations while maintaining the same family values that built the business.

"It's not just about scrap metal - it's about building lives and communities."

- The Buzbee Family

1990s

Industrial Scale Operations

K&L pioneers mobile crushing technology and expands into industrial demolition services, becoming East Texas's trusted partner for large-scale operations.

"We invest in our people and our equipment so we can serve our customers better."

- The Buzbee Family

2010s

Sustainability Leadership

Embracing advanced recycling technologies and environmental certifications, K&L becomes a regional leader in sustainable scrap metal processing.

"Every piece of metal we recycle is a step toward a cleaner tomorrow."

- The Buzbee Family

2024

56 Years Strong

Three generations continue the legacy of excellence, community service, and fair dealing that started in 1956.

"We're not just in the recycling business - we're in the relationship business."

- The Buzbee Family
